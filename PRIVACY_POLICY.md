# Privacy Policy for Anjra

**Last Updated: January 28, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Anjra** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children and families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information to provide our services:

*   **Parent Data**: We may collect your email address and name when you create a parent account to manage your family's finances.
*   **Child Data**: Parents may create profiles for their children (e.g., nicknames, age). We do not collect personal contact information (like phone numbers or emails) directly from children under 13 without parental consent.
*   **Financial Data**: We track virtual allowances, chore completion, and mock transaction history within the app. **Anjra does not link to real bank accounts** and does not process real money transactions. All balances are virtual/educational.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data solely for the following purposes:
*   To provide and maintain the App's functionality (tracking chores and allowances).
*   To authenticate users and secure accounts.
*   To improve our services and fix bugs.

**We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.**

We implement appropriate security measures (encryption and secure database storage via Supabase) to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of your personal information.
